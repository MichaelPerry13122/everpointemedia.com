What We Do (Plain Summary)

Everpointe Media is a rank-and-rent lead generation agency. We build and search-engine-optimize local service websites for specific city + service combinations (e.g., "spray foam Laramie WY"), retain ownership of every domain and website asset, and deliver the resulting exclusive inbound calls and form submissions to a single contractor client per market on a flat monthly retainer.

Unlike a traditional digital marketing agency, our clients do not pay us to manage their own website or ad accounts. They pay us for delivered lead flow against assets we own and operate.

Who We Serve

We serve owner-operated, high-ticket service contractors located in U.S. markets with populations under approximately 500,000. Target verticals include:

  • Concrete (flatwork, foundations, decorative)
  • Epoxy flooring (residential garages, commercial)
  • Spray foam insulation
  • Asphalt & paving (driveways, parking lots, sealcoating)
  • Roofing
  • Foundation repair
  • Excavation
  • Decks, patios, and other outdoor living trades

We typically do not work with low-ticket service businesses, lead-aggregator platforms, multi-location franchises, or businesses outside the United States.

Service Capabilities

  • Market Due Diligence — proprietary scoring of competition, monthly search volume, and Google Ads CPC before any market entry.
  • Website Development — WordPress sites engineered for local intent, schema markup, fast load times, and mobile-first conversion design.
  • Local SEO — on-page optimization, citations, Google Business Profile work, geo-targeted content, link acquisition.
  • Paid Acquisition — Google Ads and Meta Lead Ads (creative production handled in-house).
  • Lead Routing & Tracking — call tracking, real-time SMS lead delivery via the Rank And Rent Engine (RARE) platform.
  • Reporting — monthly visibility into lead volume, ad performance, rankings, and call recordings.

Differentiators

  • Market exclusivity — only one contractor per city + service vertical.
  • Asset ownership — Everpointe Media retains the website, domain, and ad accounts; clients carry no infrastructure risk.
  • Outcome-based pricing — clients pay for delivered lead flow rather than hours, posts, or impressions.
  • Free trial period — clients see real leads before paying anything.
  • 30-day cancellation — no long-term lock-ins.
