We're Built Different — and So Is the Lead Flow.

Most marketing agencies sell you services. We sell you leads. You don't pay for hours, ad spend, or "strategy" — you pay one flat fee and we deliver paying customers in your market.

The Story

Everpointe Media was founded by Michael Perry in Provo, Utah. We're built around a simple truth most agencies dodge: contractors don't want websites, social posts, or "brand awareness." They want their phone to ring.

So we built a model around making that happen — and only that. We invest our own capital to research, build, and rank websites for specific service-and-city combinations like "spray foam Laramie WY" or "epoxy flooring Helena MT." We own the asset. You rent the lead flow. One contractor per market. No bidding wars, no shared leads, no budgets to manage.

Why "Rank & Rent" Wins

  • Aligned incentives. We don't get paid unless the asset performs. If leads stop, you stop paying — simple as that.
  • Predictable pricing. One flat monthly fee. No ad-spend management games, no surprise invoices.
  • Real exclusivity. Your competitors are not on our roster in your city. Period.
  • You don't carry the risk. The site is ours, the SEO is ours, the ad accounts are ours. You take delivery of leads.

How We're Different From a Typical Agency
